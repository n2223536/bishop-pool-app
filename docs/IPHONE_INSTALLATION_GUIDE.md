# 📱 Bishop Estates Cabana Club - iPhone Installation Guide

## Quick Start: Add to Home Screen (iPhone 6+)

### What is this?
The Bishop Estates Cabana Club app is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** — it works like a native app on your iPhone, but through your web browser. It works offline, loads instantly, and updates automatically.

### System Requirements
- **iPhone 6 or newer** ✓
- **iOS 12.2 or later** ✓
- **Safari browser** (included)
- **Internet connection** (for first load only)

---

## Step-by-Step Installation

### 1️⃣ Open Safari
Open Safari on your iPhone and navigate to:
```
https://cabanaclub.example.com
```
(Replace with your actual URL)

### 2️⃣ Tap Share
Tap the **Share button** at the bottom of your screen (looks like a box with an arrow ⬆️).

> **Can't find it?** It's at the bottom right of Safari:
> - **iPhone 6-X/11/12 (etc):** Bottom right corner
> - **iPhone 6+:** Bottom center
> - **iPhone 14 Pro:** Bottom center

### 3️⃣ Scroll Down & Select "Add to Home Screen"
In the share menu, scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"** (you may need to scroll left/right to find it).

### 4️⃣ Name Your App (Optional)
You'll see a dialog with the app name. Edit it if you like, or just tap **"Add"**.

### 5️⃣ Done! 🎉
The app icon now appears on your home screen. Tap it to launch!

## Troubleshooting

### "Add to Home Screen" Option Not Showing?

**Try this:**
1. Make sure you're using **Safari** (not Chrome or another browser)
2. Close Safari completely and reopen it
3. Visit the site again
4. Try the Share button again

**Still missing?** The app may not meet requirements. Check:
- [ ] iOS 12.2 or later (Settings → General → About)
- [ ] You're viewing HTTPS (not HTTP)
- [ ] Internet connection is active

### App Won't Load or Keeps Refreshing

**Solutions:**
1. **Force close the app:** Swipe up from the bottom edge to close it
2. **Close Safari cache:** Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data
3. **Check internet:** Try loading in regular Safari first
4. **Restart your iPhone:** Power off and back on

### The Icon Shows Web Browser Instead of App Icon

**This is normal!** On older iOS, it uses Safari's icon. When you use the app in full-screen mode, it feels like a native app.

To ensure the real icon shows:
- Delete the app and re-add it (Steps 1-5 above)
- iOS may update the icon after you use the app a few times

### Notifications Aren't Working

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**
2. Find **Safari** in the list
3. Ensure **Allow Notifications** is turned ON

### Can I Delete the App?

Yes! Just like any other iPhone app:
1. Press and hold the app icon
2. Tap **"Remove App"**
3. Confirm

You can reinstall anytime by following the steps above.

## What's a PWA?

A **Progressive Web App (PWA)** is a modern web application that works like a native app. It:
- ✅ Works offline
- ✅ Installs on your home screen
- ✅ Loads instantly
- ✅ Sends notifications
- ✅ Uses device features (camera, location, etc.)

Think of it as the best of web + native apps, without needing the App Store!
